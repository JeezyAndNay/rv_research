# Alliance RV Delta 281BH & 284RK - Maintenance Product List
*Date: 2026-05-22*

Every product chosen for a specific reason tied to the Delta's documented failure modes or warranty language. Alternatives listed where the primary isn't available locally.

---

## Sealants
*Most critical category for Alliance warranty protection*

Document sealant by name in your maintenance log. "RV sealant" is not a defensible answer if Alliance questions your maintenance.

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dicor 501LSW Self-Leveling Lap Sealant** (white or gray) | Horizontal roof surfaces - flows into joints automatically | Amazon, Camping World, any RV supply | ~$12/tube |
| **Dicor 505LSW Non-Sag Lap Sealant** (white or gray) | Vertical sidewall seams, corner caps, around exterior lights | Same as above | ~$12/tube |
| **Dicor RP-RC-1 Roof Cleaner** | Cleaning rubber roof before re-sealing - safe for TPO/EPDM | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Eternabond RoofSeal Tape** (white, 4" wide) | Emergency field repair and reinforcement - bonds immediately, lasts years | Amazon, Camping World | ~$25/roll |

### Sealant Notes
- Buy 2 tubes of 501LSW per season minimum - one for spring, one for fall
- Old Dicor that's skinned over won't flow correctly - check sell date on tube
- Keep one tube of 505LSW in the trailer at all times for field emergencies
- **Do NOT use:** silicone caulk (won't bond to Azdel/fiberglass), Flex Seal products, automotive weather-stripping adhesive

## Azdel-Specific Hardware
*Most Alliance-specific section on the list*

Standard wood screws don't work well in Azdel composite. When remounting anything that's pulled out, you need anchors designed for composite panels.

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **E-Z Ancor Twist-N-Lock 50 Drywall Anchors** | Mounting in Azdel - grips composite better than standard screws | Home Depot, Lowe's, Amazon | ~$8/pack |
| **Toggler SNAP TOGGLE Anchors** | Heavier loads in Azdel - TV bracket, heavy shelf remounting | Home Depot, Lowe's, Amazon | ~$12/pack |
| **3M VHB Double-Sided Tape** (1/2" and 1" widths) | Supplemental adhesion for trim pieces, backsplash - documented trim-over-slide failure | Amazon, Home Depot | ~$12 |
| **Gorilla Heavy Duty Construction Adhesive** | Rebonding delaminating backsplash - documented bubbling issue on Alliance Deltas | Amazon, Home Depot, Lowe's | ~$8 |

### Azdel Mounting Notes
- Never re-drive a screw into the same Azdel hole that stripped - it will strip again immediately
- Correct repair: clean hole, fill with two-part epoxy (PC-11 Marine Epoxy), let cure fully, re-drill, then insert anchor
- For backsplash bubbling: inject construction adhesive behind bubble with syringe, press flat, weight overnight
