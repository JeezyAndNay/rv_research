# Alliance RV Delta 281BH & 284RK - PDI Checklist
*Built from documented owner issues on alliancervowners.com, iRV2, RV Insider, and cartipsdaily.com*
*Date: 2026-05-22*

Items marked **🔴 CRITICAL** have been documented as factory-installed defects - expect them on your unit until proven otherwise.

---

## Solar & Electrical
*281BH 🔴 | 284RK - verify*

- [ ] **🔴 Test solar output at the MPPT charge controller before leaving the lot**
  - Documented 2025 factory defect on 281BH: solar leads wired to a single 6V battery instead of both in series
  - MPPT tries to charge a 6V system; 0.0VDC at controller PV ports
  - Should read ~44VDC from rooftop panels at PV input terminals on a sunny day
- [ ] Verify battery bank is wired in series (12V system), not parallel or single-battery
  - Check the pass-through storage bay where the battery disconnect switch lives
- [ ] Inspect the butt-connector splice behind the electrical panel joining two PV wires - should be weatherproof and secure
- [ ] Test all 110V outlets - plug an outlet tester into every single one
- [ ] **🔴 Verify GFCI outlet wiring for washer/dryer connections**
  - 2025 owner found 20-amp GFCI breaker wired incorrectly at factory
  - Trip the GFCI, verify it resets and correct outlets go dead/live
- [ ] Test all USB charging ports throughout
- [ ] Verify all coax fittings - factory ships them barely finger-tight; tighten every one by hand

---

## Slides
*281BH: 1 slide | 284RK: 2 slides 🔴*

- [ ] **Run the slide(s) out and back in at least 3 full cycles** - listen for straining, one-sided walking, or overload stops engaging
- [ ] **🔴 Check slide flooring edges after extending** - flooring pulling up at one end creating a tripping hazard is documented
- [ ] Inspect underside of slide for scarring marks (contact with something during travel)
- [ ] Examine Azdel wall where slide trim attaches - push on all slide trim to feel for looseness
- [ ] **🔴 Verify tank dump handle access with slides OUT**
  - Both floor plans position black and gray dump handles under the slide
  - Confirm whether handles are reachable without retracting - this is a known design issue
- [ ] 284RK: test both slides independently, confirm 55" TV pivots smoothly with both slides out

---

## Plumbing & Water

- [ ] **Connect to city water and pressurize before leaving the lot** - walk entire trailer listening and looking for drips
- [ ] **🔴 Test the shower door seal thoroughly**
  - Run water for 2-3 minutes, check bathroom floor outside the shower pan
  - Documented across Delta models: shower leaks around and under the door
- [ ] Check all tank remote drain valve handles - pull each and verify the actual valve moves at the tank
- [ ] Turn on every individual water fixture shutoff valve - verify each one actually shuts off its fixture
- [ ] Run the tankless water heater until hot water arrives at every faucet
- [ ] Test black and gray tank flush if equipped - stripped thread/valve leak documented on initial use
- [ ] Confirm monitor panel operation with dealer
  - 281BH: get explanation of two grey tank buttons (2026 models) - not covered in manual

---

## Interior Fit & Finish - Azdel Mounting
*Both models 🔴*

- [ ] **🔴 Push on every shelf, cabinet door, and wall-mounted component**
  - Azdel doesn't grip screws - shelf under TV has pulled mounting screws out of wall
  - Apply gentle outward pressure to every mounted item
- [ ] Open and close every cabinet door - check for full closure and soft-close function
- [ ] Open and close every drawer - check bunkhouse under-bed drawers latch and stay closed
- [ ] Check backsplash in kitchen for bubbling/delamination - press along full surface
- [ ] Pull on all towel bar/ring mounts
- [ ] Walk the full vinyl floor looking for screws or fasteners coming up through the surface
- [ ] Open and close the bedroom sliding door (281BH) or bedroom door (284RK) - check for binding, verify lock functions
- [ ] **281BH specific:** test bunkhouse access ladder for stability - pull and side-load it. Verify each bunk's light and outlet work
- [ ] **284RK specific:** verify pantry pull-out tray slides fully and stop mechanism holds it. Test tip-out trash can

---

## Exterior & Roof Seals

- [ ] **Walk the entire roof** - inspect all lap sealant around vents, A/C units, and antenna base for cracks or gaps
- [ ] Inspect all exterior seals at corners, seams, and slide toppers for separations
- [ ] **🔴 Photograph every exterior seal on all four sides with date-stamp before leaving the lot**
  - Alliance warranty excludes water damage from "inadequate seal maintenance" even when root cause is factory defect
  - Day-1 photos are your warranty defense
- [ ] Test powered 20-foot awning: deploy fully, verify end caps seated, check fabric, verify retraction
- [ ] Check all exterior storage compartment doors and latches
- [ ] Verify propane tank enclosure latches securely
- [ ] Verify the exterior entry door(s) close and latch cleanly
  - 281BH has two: front bedroom exterior door and main entry

---

## Appliances

- [ ] Run both A/C units to cold - verify both compressors kick on and each reaches cold air
- [ ] Test the 3-burner cooktop - light all three burners, check ignitors work and flames hold
- [ ] Run the oven if equipped - verify it heats and door seal is intact
- [ ] Verify refrigerator is at temperature (turn on when arriving at dealership, allow time to cool)
- [ ] Test range hood fan on all speeds
- [ ] Test the exterior shower if equipped

| GVWR | 8,850 lbs | Verify at dealer |
| Hitch weight | 721 lbs | Verify at dealer |
| Cargo capacity | ~2,068 lbs | Verify at dealer |

- [ ] Get actual hitch weight measured at dealer or truck scale
- [ ] **284RK:** verify tow vehicle payload and tow rating against actual loaded weight - 7,536 lbs dry pushes most half-tons
- [ ] Verify all lug nuts are torqued - ask dealer to confirm with torque wrench
- [ ] Inspect tires - sidewalls for date codes (within 2 years) and cracking
- [ ] Test all running lights, brake lights, and turn signals via tow vehicle
- [ ] Verify breakaway cable is attached and test the breakaway switch

## Documentation - Do This Before Signing

- [ ] **Get a signed PDI form from the dealer with every completed item checked**
- [ ] **Photograph every exterior seal, all four sides, plus the roof** - date-stamped, before moving the trailer
- [ ] Get the full written warranty document - read the water damage exclusion clause carefully
- [ ] Register trailer with Alliance directly on the day of delivery to start warranty clock correctly
- [ ] Ask the dealer: "If I need a warranty repair in 60 days, what's your typical turnaround for parts from Alliance?"
- [ ] Get dealer service department direct contact (not sales desk)
- [ ] Confirm dealer has Alliance-authorized technicians on staff
- [ ] Confirm monitor panel layout with dealer (especially 281BH two-grey-tank button)

---

## Print-and-Bring Summary - 10 Must-Test Items

1. MPPT solar output (281BH) - 44VDC at PV terminals on a sunny day
2. Slide(s) - 3 full cycles, listen for strain, check flooring edges
3. City water pressurized - walk every inch looking for drips
4. Shower door seal - 2-3 minutes running, check floor outside pan
5. GFCI washer/dryer outlets - trip and reset
6. Every Azdel-mounted shelf/component - gentle outward pressure
7. Both A/C units to cold
8. All drain valve handles physically connected and moving
9. Photograph every exterior seal before moving the unit
10. Get signed PDI form and full warranty document before signing
