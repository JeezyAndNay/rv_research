### 5 Factory Defects to Verify at PDI

These are **documented real incidents**, not theoretical risks. Verify all five before you sign:

1. **🔴 281BH Solar wiring** — leads wired to a single 6V battery instead of both batteries in series. The 200W solar panel produces zero output until corrected. Test: ~44VDC at MPPT controller PV input terminals on a sunny day. Zero volts = defect.

2. **🔴 GFCI wiring (both models)** — 20-amp GFCI breaker for washer/dryer outlets wired incorrectly at the factory. Test: trip and reset each GFCI, verify the correct outlets go dead/live.

3. **🔴 Shower door seal (both models)** — documented leak around and under the door. Test: run shower 3 full minutes, check the bathroom floor outside the shower pan.

4. **🔴 Drain valve handles not connected (both models)** — handles present but cable not attached to valve. Test: pull each handle and physically verify the valve moves at the tank.

5. **🔴 Azdel wall mounting failures (both models)** — Azdel composite doesn't grip screws. Shelves, trim, and brackets work loose. Test: push gently outward on every wall-mounted component before signing.

## The Warranty Trap

Alliance's warranty excludes water damage caused by **"inadequate seal maintenance"** — even when a factory defect caused the original breach.

**How to protect yourself:**
- Photograph every exterior seal on all four sides plus the roof **before leaving the lot** (date-stamped)
- Inspect and re-photograph seals every spring and fall
- Log every inspection with dates and product names
- Keep receipts for sealant purchases

A dated photo log twice a year is your defense if Alliance ever denies a water damage claim.
