## Key Findings

### 281BH Has the Stronger Owner Community Presence

The 281BH generates actual discussion on the [Alliance RV Owners Forum](https://alliancervowners.com/forum/forums/alliance-delta-general-discussion.48/), gets dedicated YouTube walkthroughs ([Tall Man's RV Reviews](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PKNglRSxT9E), [2026 tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6xDcOvtWv8M)), and a detailed [Bish's RV editorial review](https://www.bishs.com/blog/alliance-rv-delta-281bh-travel-trailer/). The 284RK has fewer forum threads and no major YouTube reviews. Buying the 281BH means more community knowledge to draw on when something goes wrong.

### 281BH Has a Documented Factory Solar Wiring Defect

A 2025 owner on the [Alliance RV Owners Forum](https://alliancervowners.com/forum/threads/new-delta-281bh-with-no-solar-output.3198/) found solar leads wired to a single 6V battery instead of using both batteries in series. The MPPT charge controller tries to charge a 6V system; the 200W solar panel produces zero output until corrected. The positive PV line also routes through a butt-connector splice behind the electrical panel.

**Test at PDI:** Measure ~44VDC at the MPPT controller PV input terminals on a sunny day. Zero volts = defect.

### Monitor Panel Issues (281BH - 2026 models)

A separate [2026 281BH monitor panel thread](https://alliancervowners.com/forum/threads/2026-281bh-delta-monitor-panel.3121/) documents the panel having two grey tank buttons not covered correctly in the owner's manual. Get the dealer to explain every button at delivery.

### 1-Slide vs 2-Slide is a Meaningful Reliability Difference

The 284RK's two slides double the exposure to documented Alliance slide issues: straining during retraction, overload stops engaging, slides walking in one side at a time, flooring pulling up at edges (tripping hazard), loose cables, and slide wall trim falling off due to Azdel screw-holding failure. The 281BH's single slide is structurally simpler with half the mechanical risk surface.

### Both Share the Azdel Screw-Holding Weakness

Azdel composite walls don't grip screws the way wood does. The result across Delta models:
- Shelf under the TV pulled its mounting screws out of the wall
- Trim over slides falls off
- Accessories can't be mounted without anchors
- Components screwed to Azdel at the factory work loose over time

This is a recurring theme on the [Problems cropping up thread](https://alliancervowners.com/forum/threads/problems-cropping-up.1284/), not isolated incidents.

### Tank Dump Handle Access Applies to Both

Both the 281BH and 284RK position tank dump handles under the slide. You must retract the slide or crawl on the ground to dump tanks. This is a design choice, not a defect - but it surprises first-time owners consistently.

### The 284RK Lineup Position - Check the 294RK First

| Model | Length | Dry Weight | Slides | Sleeps | Key differentiator |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 274RKW | 31'11" | 7,420 lbs | 2 | 3 | Shorter, slightly lighter |
| **284RK** | **32'11"** | **7,536 lbs** | **2** | **3** | More counter space |
| 294RK | 33'11" | 7,316 lbs | 2 | 4 | Outdoor griddle + entertainment, lighter despite longer |

The 294RK is a full foot longer than the 284RK but 220 lbs lighter and sleeps one more, with an outdoor griddle station added. If you're buying a rear kitchen Delta, compare the 294RK side-by-side before committing to the 284RK.

## Key Patterns from the Research

1. **281BH is better documented** - more forum threads, more YouTube reviews, more owner voices. Per [RV Insider](https://www.rvinsider.com/Alliance-Rv-Delta-281Bh-281BH-RV-Reviews?make=Alliance+Rv&model=Delta+281Bh&floorplan=281BH)
2. **1-slide vs 2-slide is a meaningful reliability split** - documented slide issues on [alliancervowners.com](https://alliancervowners.com/forum/) make the 281BH's single slide structurally simpler
3. **Solar wiring defect specific to 281BH** - verify at delivery per [New Delta 281BH with no solar output](https://alliancervowners.com/forum/threads/new-delta-281bh-with-no-solar-output.3198/)
4. **PDI checklist items for both:** tank dump handle access, all Azdel-mounted components for tightness, solar output at MPPT, shower door seal, slide flooring edges, all coax connections
5. **The 294RK undercuts the 284RK** on weight despite being longer and more featured - per [Alliance RV Delta lineup](https://alliancerv.com/travel-trailer-delta)

## Warranty Warning (Both Models)

Per the [Warranty concerns thread](https://alliancervowners.com/forum/threads/warranty-concerns.2748/) and [What's Reasonable for Warranty thread](https://alliancervowners.com/forum/threads/whats-reasonable-for-warranty.2615/):

- Alliance carries no spare parts inventory - all components go to production first
- Warranty parts are made on demand after approval - multi-month waits documented
- One owner reported 3 months in service within 6 months of ownership
- Alliance's warranty excludes water damage from "inadequate seal maintenance" even when a factory defect caused the breach

**Document everything at delivery with dated photos. Register your warranty the day of delivery.**
