# Grand Design Imagine — Maintenance Product List
*2970RL · 2810BH · 2800BH*
*Date: 2026-05-23*

Products chosen specifically for the Imagine's construction: one-piece TPO rubber roof, aluminum-framed laminated walls, OSB subfloor, Schwintek in-wall slides, and standard Dexter axle/drum brake setup.

## Sealants
*The most important annual spend on any rubber-roof travel trailer*

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dicor 501LSW Self-Leveling Lap Sealant** (white) | All horizontal roof surfaces: A/C bases, vent rims, MaxxAir fan collars, roof-to-wall perimeter | Amazon, Camping World | ~$12/tube |
| **Dicor 505LSW Non-Sag Lap Sealant** (white) | All vertical applications: sidewall seams, corner caps, window trim, front cap seam | Amazon, Camping World | ~$12/tube |
| **Dicor RP-RC-1 Rubber Roof Cleaner** | Clean TPO roof before re-sealing — removes road grime and old oxidized sealant | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Dicor RP-CRP-1 Rubber Roof Conditioner/Protectant** | UV protection for TPO membrane — apply after cleaning, annually | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Eternabond RoofSeal Tape** (4" white) | Emergency field repair for any roof breach — bonds aggressively to TPO | Amazon, Camping World | ~$25/roll |

### Notes
- Buy 2 tubes of 501LSW and 2 tubes of 505LSW per season — use them
- Keep 1 tube of each in the trailer for field repairs
- **2970RL:** buy an extra tube of 501LSW for the rear wall-to-roof seam — the documented high-failure zone
- The TPO rubber roof is the #1 failure point if sealant maintenance is skipped. OSB subfloor + water = expensive repair.

## Rubber Roof Maintenance
*TPO-specific — different from fiberglass-exterior trailers*

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dicor RP-RC-1 Roof Cleaner** (also above) | Annual wash of full TPO roof surface | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Dicor RP-CRP-1 Roof Conditioner** | UV protectant for TPO rubber — annual application after cleaning | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **303 Aerospace Protectant** (large spray) | UV protection for rubber moldings, slide seals, and entry door seals | Amazon, auto parts, Camping World | ~$15 |

### Notes
- Do NOT use petroleum-based products, citrus cleaners, or harsh solvents on TPO rubber — they cause premature deterioration
- Do NOT use a pressure washer directly on roof sealants
- 303 Aerospace is used on both the rubber roof AND slide seals — one product, two uses

## Slide Maintenance
*Schwintek in-wall slide system*

| Product | Use | Where to Buy | ~Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **303 Aerospace Protectant** (listed above) | Rubber wiper seal conditioning — twice per season minimum | Amazon, Camping World | ~$15 |
| **Protect All Slide-Out Seal Treatment** | Alternative to 303 for slide rubber seals | Camping World, Amazon | ~$12 |
| **Camco Slide-Out Dry Lube** | Schwintek slide track lubrication — silicone-based, does not attract dirt | Amazon, Camping World, Walmart | ~$10 |

### Notes
- **Do NOT use WD-40, white lithium grease, or petroleum lubricants on Schwintek tracks** — they attract debris that can jam the motor
- Camco Dry Lube (silicone-based spray) is the correct product for the Schwintek system
- Clean tracks with a dry cloth before each lubrication — grit in the track is the primary cause of Schwintek motor strain errors
