## Quick Reference - Most Critical Facts

### Documented Factory Defects (Verify at PDI)
1. **281BH solar wiring** - leads wired to single 6V battery instead of batteries in series. Test: ~44VDC at MPPT controller PV terminals on sunny day
2. **GFCI wiring** - 20-amp GFCI breaker for washer/dryer outlets wired incorrectly. Test: trip and reset each GFCI, verify correct outlets go dead/live
3. **Shower door seal** - leaks around and under door on Delta models broadly. Test: run 3 minutes, check bathroom floor outside pan
4. **Drain valve handles not connected** - pull each handle and verify the actual valve moves at the tank
5. **Azdel mounting failures** - push on every wall-mounted component before signing

### Warranty Trap to Know
Alliance excludes water damage from "inadequate seal maintenance" even when a factory defect caused the breach. Photograph every exterior seal before leaving the lot. Maintain a dated inspection log with photos twice per year.

### 281BH vs 284RK at a Glance

| | 281BH | 284RK |
|---|---|---|
| Best for | Families, sleeping capacity | Couples, serious cooking |
| Dry weight | ~6,782 lbs | ~7,536 lbs |
| Slides | 1 | 2 |
| Sleeps | 8 | 3 |
| MSRP | ~$59,910 | ~$60,139 |
| Reliability edge | Yes - 1 slide, more documented | 2 slides = more failure surface |
| Community data | More forum threads, YouTube reviews | Thinner online presence |

### Before You Sign - Non-Negotiables
1. Signed PDI form with all findings documented
2. Any "fix before delivery" items resolved OR scheduled in writing before you take the unit
3. Dated photos of every exterior seal on all four sides plus roof
4. Full warranty document in hand - read the water damage exclusion clause
5. Alliance warranty registered on delivery day
6. Service department direct contact info (not sales)
